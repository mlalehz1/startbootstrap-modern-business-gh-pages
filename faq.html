Medgen EHR is certified by Drummond Group as well as Meaningful Use compliant. Providers will be directed to a link on the CMS website where they will register their EHR usage. Medicare and Medicaid providers can prepare for Stimulus incentives to obtain an NPI number. They must register in the National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES). Go to https://nppes.cms.hhs.gov/NPPES/Welcome.do for an explanation on NPPES and instructions for registering. Skip this step if you already have an NPI. If you are a Medicare provider and have an existing NPI, you can then go to https://pecos.cms.hhs.gov to register with PECOS (Provider Enrolment Chain and Ownership System), if you have not done so already. Only Medicare providers must register with PECOS. Medicaid providers need not register.
